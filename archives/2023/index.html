About me

I am Kai Cao, a sophomore at the University of California, Irvine, pursuing a major in Biological Science. I am passionate about learning and research in the fields of neuroscience and biotechnology. I am currently in Igarashi’s Lab at UCI School of Medicine supervised by Dr. Kei Igarashi.

I have participated in various academic programs and projects that have enhanced my skills and knowledge. For example, I am working as a learning assistant for Chem 1B General Chemistry at UCI, where I help other students improve their understanding of the subject and facilitate communication with the professor. I also conducted a comprehensive pathology analysis and designed an experiment related to Alzheimer’s disease as a group leader in high school, demonstrating my critical thinking and data analysis abilities.

I am always eager to learn new things and challenge myself. I am looking for opportunities to apply my skills and interests in meaningful ways and contribute to the advancement of science and society.

I am currently seeking a research opportunity or internship in the areas of Neuroscience and Cell Biology.
